WHAT WE DO

We offer premium traditional hair treatments including our 'free from' menu featuring products that are up to 90% natural, sulfate-free, silicone-free, vegan and cruelty free.

Putting hair health first, our range of organic and bespoke treatments will compliment your flawless finish.

ABOUT

My dad said you better get an education and suggested vocational school to be a hairdresser. From there, you could say it happened organically. Sophia Avera had a knack for hairdressing right from the start.

Her enthusiasm for the industry is fueled by the creativity and challenge of exceeding each client's expectations. No matter if the client is a person in her chair or in her classroom, Sophia strives to make sure they have an exceptional experience.

With 20+ years of experience behind the chair, and as a National Educator / Partner with L'Oreal's Soho and LA academies, Sophia has the skills, passion, and vision to create a salon experience unlike any other. She is thrilled to be realizing her dream by opening SASS and is best known for hair that grows out well, whether it be haircuts, hair color or hair extensions.
